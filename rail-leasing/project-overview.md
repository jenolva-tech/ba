# Rail Leasing Management System

## Key
RAIL

## Summary
The Rail Leasing Management System is an application designed to streamline the end to end lifecycle of railcar leasing operations.  The system supports customer information management, sales transaction, contract management, fleet management, maintenance management and billing.  It enables management of assets efficiently, reduce risks and improve visibility and collaboration.

## Business Problem
Fragmented systems and manual processes leads to data inconsistencies, delayed decision making and compliance risks.  The Rail Leasing Management system centralizes these workflows into a unified platform.

## Goals
    - Digitize and automate the rail leasing lifecycle
    - Improve accuracy of contract and billing data
    - Provide vsisiblity of fleet utilization
    - Reduce manual effort and oeprational bottlenecks
    - Support compliance with industry and regulatory standards

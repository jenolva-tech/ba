# Contract Management

# Key
RAIL-Contract-Management

## Summary
Provide end to end functionalities to create, update and maintain leasing contracts within the Rail Leasing Management System  This epic ensures contract data is accurate, compliant and accessible for downstream processes such as billing, fleet management, maintenance management.

## Objective
    - To have a main system to record all information of a leasing document
    - Have a system or record that can easily generate a lease contract

## Stakeholders
    - Contract Analyst
    - Department Supervisor
    - Rail Management User
